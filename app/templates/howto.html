How it works

Create a New Track

Navigate to the New Track page. Enter the Name of Track (i.e. Single Ladies) and Original Artist (i.e. Beyoncé). These two fields will help other users looking to collaborate on specific songs find your track. Selecting Hidden will prevent your track from turning up when searched. However, it will still be accessible from your account and by anyone with the URL. This feature is useful for private collaborations. Finally, to create the track you can either record directly from your browser by hitting Add Recording for New Track or you can upload a music file to start the track by selecting Upload Audio to Start Track

If you select Add Recording for New Track you will hear a 3 second countdown, after which the recording will begin. Once you are done recording, select Add Recording for New Track again to stop recording. The following waveform will then be displayed. Hit Space to play the recording and decide if you are satisfied with it. If you are not, you many Delete Recording and try again, or Save Recording as a new track.
